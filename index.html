[ About ]

Hello everyone, I'm Martin, also known as DocMcFly. I am a hobbyist and self-taught concept designer with a professional background in CAx technology and systems engineering. My work is influenced by 90's video games and Japanese internet pop culture. From a design perspective, I adhere to the principles of minimalism, particularly the design ethos of Japanese Industrial Designer Kenya Hara. Sometimes, I prototype my own industrial design ideas using my CNC milling machine. I do not follow a specific roadmap for my projects; instead, I often experiment with different tools and techniques. This website showcases my projects in concept design, graphics, coding, and prototyping.
